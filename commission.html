Terms of Service

  • Please fill out my commission sheet to make a request. I will not be accepting requests through DMs or emails.

  • All prices are in USD.

  • All commission work is for personal use only. If you intend to post it on any platform do not remove my signature and be sure to give credit.


  • No refunds once I start working on your commission. If you think there's a possibility that you might need that money for something else please do not continue with your request.

  • I have a right to refuse potentially disturbing commission requests. There may be something you want drawn that isn't explicitly stated under the "I will not draw" section. However, I will still turn down any form of problematic or uncomfortable requests.

  • Send me all the necessary references. Poses, clothing, weapons, etc., I will accept a picrew or a photobash of your character or content of commission if available. Minecraft and Roblox references are accepted however it may require multiple screenings of the sketch.

  • 50% downpayment should be paid. Once I send you a sketch that you are satisfied with. If you're unable to comply then I will cancel your commission after 3 days.
