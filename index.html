About Me

I go by Francie online; a name I use across my creative and technical work. Francie serves as a brand for my technology related explorations, while this portfolio represents my broader creative practice.

My real name is Alessandra Delos Santos, I'm a BSIT student with a stronger pull towards creative work; particularly video editing, graphic design and visual storytelling.

Alongside my creative work, I also explore IT-related projects such as web development and game development. While these aren't my main focus, they help shape my understanding of structure, systems, and interaction, which benefits my creative process.

I’m currently seeking opportunities in the creative industry, applying for video and creative roles, while continuing to grow my technical experience through personal and academic projects. I'm open to opportunities where creativity is valued and where there is room to grow across disciplines.

Video Editing

I prioritize clarity first, making sure the story is understood before anything else. From there, I shape emotion and atmosphere, using pacing and rhythm to support the narrative. While my work leans toward structured storytelling, I occasionally explore experimental ideas when they serve the piece; keeping what works and discarding what doesn't.

Graphic Design

I think visually first, but I design best when a concept leads the visuals. My comfort space is bold, reduced, compositions; as shown best in my personal works, but I'm actively refining my process to ensure every visual decision is grounded in intent.

Creative Tech

I keep technology in my skill set to expand the range of creative opportunities. I don't treat tech as separate from creativity; I view it as a way to build, control, and extend ideas beyond traditional mediums.
